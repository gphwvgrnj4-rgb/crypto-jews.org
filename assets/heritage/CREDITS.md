## Notes on accuracy
- *Friday Evening* is a Central European (Ashkenazi) Sabbath scene, not an
  Iberian one. It is used because it depicts the candle-lighting practice the
  article describes, and the caption says so explicitly rather than implying it
  is Sephardic.
- The Berruguete and Otzolotepec paintings depict Inquisition scenes; the
  Otzolotepec one is colonial Mexico, matching the Latin America article.
